# What is AI Engineering? (The Definition of the New Profession)

AI Engineering is the discipline of **building, deploying, and maintaining** software systems that use artificial intelligence. Particularly large language models (LLMs) and other ML models. To solve problems in the real world.


**Role Comparison: AI Engineer vs. Data Scientist and ML Engineer**


| Role | Main Purpose | Focus Areas |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Data Scientist | Finding Patterns and Insights. | Statistics, experiments, and data cleaning. |
| ML Engineer | Building and training specific models. | Algorithm design, mathematics, and model architecture. |
| AI Engineer | Developing AI-based products. | Integration, APIs, Agents, and user experience. |



**Note the Difference:**

We're not here to invent a new mathematical algorithm (that's the ML Engineer's job) and not to clean data for reports (that's the Data Scientist). We're here to be **AI Engineers**. Those who "close the gaps" and build a product that works. Our focus is **integration**: how to connect the brain (LLM) to the body (the product) and to the environment (APIs).
