Here's an analysis of the main milestones:

**AI history is divided into three main periods:** the birth, the "winter," and the big bang of Deep Learning.


| Year | Key Event | Engineering Significance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1962 | Artificial Neural Networks (Cornell) | Birth of the concept. Models were tiny (only 1,000 parameters). |
| 2006 | AWS launch and release of CUDA (Nvidia) GPU-based which we'll discuss later | The infrastructure revolution: for the first time there's computing power available in the cloud and the ability to run parallel computations on graphics cards. |
| 2012 | AlexNet wins ImageNet | Start of the Deep Learning revolution. The model jumps to 60 million parameters. |
| 2017 | Transformer paper (Google/DeepMind) | The turning point: invention of the Transformer architecture that allows models to "understand" context (Attention). |
| 2018-2020 | GPT and BERT model families | Huge jump: from 117 million (GPT-1) to 175 billion parameters (GPT-3). 1,500x in two years! |
| 2022 | ChatGPT release (GPT 3.5) | The generative revolution becomes mainstream (100 million users in two months). |
| 2023 | The war over Comms (MSFT, Google, Meta) | AI becomes a work tool in Bing, Bard, and open models like Llama. |
| 2024 | Death of Bard and birth of Gemini | Google unifies infrastructures. The transition from a model that "answers" to a Native multi-modal model (video, audio, and text in the same model). |
| 2024 | The Small Language Models (SLMs) revolution | Release of small models like Phi and Llama 3. Understanding that you don't always need a "5-ton hammer" (GPT-5.4) for every screw. |
| 2025 | Agentic Era & MCP | The transition from Chat bots to autonomous agents. Release of the MCP protocol as a standard for data connection. |
| 2025 | On-Device AI | Deep integration of AI in operating system cores (Apple Intelligence, Windows Copilot+). Models run locally. |
| 2026 (now) | Reasoning Models & Physical AI | Models that "think" before they answer (like o1's descendants). Huge emphasis on Sovereign AI (private clouds for countries and companies). |



**Constructive skepticism:** The slide we received in the course stops at 2023 because it focuses on Fundamentals.

But in 2026, the Fundamentals have changed.

**Don't invest in Prompt Engineering:** The 2026 models already understand you even if the prompt is bad.

1. **Invest in Context Engineering:** How to provide the agent with the right data at the right time (this is what MCP solves).

2. **Think Small:** In 2026, the wisdom is not to use the biggest model, but the **smallest and most efficient** model that does the job with zero Latency.
