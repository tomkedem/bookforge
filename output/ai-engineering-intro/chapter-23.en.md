# Artificial Intelligence - 1,000 Doctors in Your Pocket.


Steve Jobs said the iPod is a thousand songs in your pocket, so AI is 1000 PhDs in your pocket, and whoever dismisses this is probably not with us today.

**The skeptical angle:**

• **Simulation of a doctorate, not a real doctorate:** The AI doesn't really hold 1,000 degrees. It holds a statistical model that knows **how to talk like** someone who has 1,000 degrees. It's excellent at synthesizing existing knowledge, but it's not capable of conducting original research or understanding the deep "why" like a human expert.

• **The danger of overconfidence:** A real doctor knows how to say "I don't know." The AI, because of the token prediction mechanism we saw in the previous slide, will always try to guess the next word. This creates a situation of "1,000 doctors who can lie with complete confidence."
