# What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)? The Foundational Definition

**"The ability of a digital computer to perform tasks commonly associated with intelligent beings."**

(Source: Encyclopedia Britannica)

<img src="/ai-engineering-intro/assets/image-05.png" alt="image-05.png" width="534" height="356" />

**What does this mean for us as a development team?**

This definition is intentionally very broad. It includes everything that seems "smart" to us, from sentence completion to trip route planning.



• **The emphasis is on the result:** The computer successfully "mimics" human cognitive function (such as: problem-solving, learning, or language understanding).

• **The transition to reality:** In this course, we turn this "capability" into a measurable engineering system.
