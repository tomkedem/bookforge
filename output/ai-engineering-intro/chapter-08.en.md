# Is AI just a hype?








Excellent question: **The distinction between Fundamentals and Buzzwords.**

The complex answer is that AI is **both** crazy hype and **deep infrastructural revolution**. Let's break it down in a cold, factual way:

**Where is the hype (The Buzzwords)?**

• **The expectation of a "magic solution":** The hype manifests in the thinking that an LLM can solve any business problem without complementary logic.

• **Wrappers:** There are thousands of startups that are just a "nice interface" on top of OpenAI's API. These are companies that will disappear the moment OpenAI adds that feature themselves.

• **Inflated valuations:** The market prices AI companies as if they're already profitable, while computing costs (Inference) are still very high.

**Note:**

Don't let newspaper headlines confuse you. There's a lot of marketing "noise" around, but **the technological capability** presented in this course. Especially in topics like **RAG** (connecting to real data) and **MLOps** (production implementation). This is real engineering.

The hype will fade, but the systems we'll learn to build here will become the new standard of the software world.
