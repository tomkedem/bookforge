# What Makes the AI Revolution Different? (The AI Revolution)

1. **Speed:**

• **The context:** The pace of progress is unprecedented. We see release of new models and technological improvements on a weekly and even daily basis.

• **The meaning for you:** What you learned yesterday may become Legacy tomorrow. This requires us as programmers to be in continuous learning.

2. **Adoption Rate:**

• **The amazing statistic:** ChatGPT reached one million users in just 5 days, and 100 million users within two months.

• **The historical context:** For comparison, it took the internet years to reach such numbers. The audience (and your customers) are adopting these tools faster than organizations can respond.

3. **Built on top of other layers:**

<img src="/ai-engineering-intro/assets/image-08.png" alt="image-08.png" width="702" height="461" />

• **The explanation:** AI couldn't have happened without the infrastructure we already built: computing power (Compute), cloud, mobile, and modern software.

• **The engineering angle:** AI is the "top floor" of the building. This means it's relatively easy for us to implement it because the infrastructure is already there.

4. **Independent decision making:**

• **The fundamental change:** This is the first time technology can replace activities that until now required human judgment only.

• **The meaning:** The transition from tools that help us (Tools) to agents that act on our behalf (Agents).
