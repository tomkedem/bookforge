# What is Generative AI? (Beyond the Dictionary Definition)

A branch of artificial intelligence capable of creating new content, such as text, images, audio, or code. By learning patterns from existing data.
<img src="/ai-engineering-intro/assets/image-07.png" alt="image-07.png" width="503" height="168" />
The illustration shows the transition from AI that analyzes and classifies (Discriminative) to AI that synthesizes (Generative). While in the past we used models to tell us "what's in the picture," today we use them to "build the picture."
