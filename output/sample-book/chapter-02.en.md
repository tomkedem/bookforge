# Chapter 2: Advanced Principles

In this chapter we will dive deeper into the advanced principles of the subject. We will learn how to apply what we learned in the first chapter.

## Practical Application

Practical application is the most critical stage. The principles can be applied in different ways:

- First way: direct and simple
- Second way: complex and in-depth
- Third way: creative and innovative

Important to remember: there is no single right way. Every person finds the approach that suits them best.
