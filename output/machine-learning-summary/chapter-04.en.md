Why we use Machine Learning:

With ML, computers can learn independently and adapt their operations according to large amounts of data.

In other words, instead of defining all the rules in advance, the system learns from the data and improves its performance over time.

The goal is to demonstrate that the system's behavior is derived from the data and not from fixed rules.
