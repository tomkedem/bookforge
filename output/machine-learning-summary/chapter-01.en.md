# Introduction

The lesson presents the fundamentals of Machine Learning within the broader context of Artificial Intelligence, aiming to build a gradual understanding of the field.

We will understand what AI actually is, where Machine Learning fits within it, why it is used, and what the big picture looks like before diving into the algorithms themselves.

The goal here is not to learn formulas or implementations, but to understand how to think correctly about Machine Learning.

The field of Machine Learning seems complex at first glance, but once you understand the big picture, everything starts to come together.
